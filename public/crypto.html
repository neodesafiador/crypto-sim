Summary



This application is a web application that allows users to simulate cryptocurrency trading.

Users are given a predetermined amount of fictitious funds. The server displays real-time prices of various cryptocurrencies. Users can buy or sell cryptocurrency based on these prices to simulate performance in the real market.

The app tracks profits and losses and provides a leader board to show the ranking of benefits that users have earned. It also includes a chart function to analyze cryptocurrency.

This app is a very useful tool for beginners and intermediate cryptocurrency traders and will be an important means of understanding market movements. Users can also test their trading strategies, minimizing risks before trading in the real market.

Overall, this app can contribute to improving the skills of cryptocurrency traders and is expected to be very useful for understanding market trends.
